MY WRITING JOURNEY

My passion for writing began in high school, where I actively participated in numerous writing competitions. Over time, my style evolved, and I developed a deep appreciation for poetic language (bahasa puitis), which led me to represent my school at the state-level Pesta Pantun competition.

This journey continued during my university years, where I became a member of the MESKOM Club—a media and communication student organization. As part of the club, I contributed articles covering campus events, student activities, and current happenings within the university community.
